Welcome To Habesha Ertib And Coffee

Are you looking for a taste of East Africa in the heart of Addis Ababa? If so, you will love Habesha Ertib, the new fast food and coffee house that offers authentic Eritrean and Ethiopian cuisine. Whether you want to enjoy a hearty breakfast, a quick lunch, or a relaxing dinner,
Habesha Ertib has something for
everyone.

At Habesha, you can choose from a variety of dishes that are made with fresh ingredients and spices. Try our signature injera, a sourdough flatbread that is served with different stews and salads. You can also sample our delicious tibs, grilled meat or vegetables with onions and peppers, or our spicy kitfo, minced beef with butter and herbs. For vegetarians and vegans, we have plenty of options, such as shiro, a chickpea stew, or timtimo, a red lentil dish. Don’t forget to pair your meal with our refreshing tej,
a honey wine, or our aromatic coffee, roasted and
brewed in the traditional way.
