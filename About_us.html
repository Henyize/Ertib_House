But we are not just a restaurant, we are also a cafe, where you can experience the Habesha coffee ceremony, a core cultural custom in Eritrea and Ethiopia. The coffee ceremony is a ritual of serving coffee daily, mainly for the purpose of getting together with relatives, neighbors, or other visitors. It involves roasting, grinding, and brewing the coffee beans over an open flame, and pouring the coffee into small cups from a height. The coffee is accompanied by a small snack, such as popcorn, peanuts, or himbasha, a traditional bread. The coffee ceremony is a symbol of hospitality, friendship, and respect, and we invite you to join us in this unique and enjoyable tradition.

Habesha Ertib and Coffee House is more than just a place to eat and drink, it is a place to connect, share, and celebrate. We are proud to share our culture and cuisine with you, and we hope you will feel at home in our cozy and friendly atmosphere. Come and visit us today, and discover the taste and spirit of Habesha.
